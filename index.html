Family mode

Quick-use references for the actual trip.

Dad tips

Use Arch Ray as the meal anchor. Use Michael Ros for the strongest photo moment. Save Six Twists for quiet bubbles.

Mom picks

Best atmosphere: Pontotoc. Best polished stop: Grape Creek. Best sunset: Michael Ros. Best easy close: Longhorn.

Baby mode

Keep the car cool, move early, bring stroller fan, prioritize A/C, and keep route changes low-friction.

Prost.

Take it slow. Drink good wine. Keep Callahan cool. Let Groot nap in the A/C.
